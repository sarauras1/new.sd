What is Reiki?

Reiki is a Japanese technique for stress reduction and relaxation that also promotes healing.

Reiki is both a spiritual practice and an energy healing system originating in Japan from its founder Mikao Usui.

It is based on the idea that an unseen life force energy flows through us and is what causes us to be alive. If one‘s life force energy is low, then we are more likely to get sick or feel stress, and if it's high we are more capable of being happy and healthy.

 Reiki treatments are performed by the practitioner laying hands-on or off the client's body, facilitating and boosting the recipient's own natural healing capabilities, by focusing energy into both the physical body and energetic field. With the help of reiki, you would be able to unblock any trapped emotion in your body.

Reiki aims to work on four levels: Physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual.
